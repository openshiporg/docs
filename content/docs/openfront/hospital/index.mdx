## Key Flows

### Patient Experience
- **Appointment Management**: Schedule appointments with preferred providers, receive reminders, complete pre-visit forms, and access telehealth consultations
- **Medical Records Access**: View test results, medication lists, treatment history, and securely share records with other healthcare providers
- **Care Coordination**: Receive treatment plans, track progress, communicate with care teams, and manage referrals to specialists

### Healthcare Operations
- **Patient Management**: Maintain HIPAA-compliant records, coordinate provider schedules, manage treatment plans, and track patient outcomes
- **Provider Coordination**: Schedule staff, manage patient load, coordinate with specialists, and ensure compliance with healthcare regulations
