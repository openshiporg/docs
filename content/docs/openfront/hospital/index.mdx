## Openfront Hospital

Openfront Hospital gives healthcare providers complete control over their patient relationships and medical data with HIPAA-compliant tools designed specifically for healthcare operations.

<Step>
### Storefront
Patient portal for appointment scheduling, medical records access, and telehealth consultations.
</Step>

<Step>
### Dashboard
Healthcare operations system for patient management, provider coordination, and billing integration.
</Step>

<Step>
### Marketplace
Connect with other healthcare providers to create referral networks and collaborative care systems.
</Step>
