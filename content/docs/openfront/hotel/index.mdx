## Key Flows

### Guest Experience
- **Room Booking**: Search availability, compare room types, select dates, and complete reservations with special requests and preferences
- **Check-in/Check-out**: Digital check-in process, room access management, billing review, and seamless departure experience
- **Guest Services**: Request amenities, book spa services, order room service, and access concierge recommendations

### Hotel Operations
- **Property Management**: Manage room inventory, coordinate housekeeping schedules, track maintenance requests, and optimize occupancy rates
- **Staff Coordination**: Assign housekeeping tasks, coordinate front desk operations, manage guest requests, and track service quality metrics
