## Key Flows

### Customer Experience
- **Smart Shopping**: Browse products with dietary filters, search by ingredients, build shopping lists, and receive personalized recommendations
- **Flexible Fulfillment**: Choose pickup, delivery, or in-store shopping with time slot selection and substitution preferences
- **Fresh Guarantee**: Track product freshness, receive expiration alerts, and access quality guarantee with easy returns

### Grocery Operations
- **Inventory Management**: Track stock levels, manage supplier relationships, coordinate deliveries, and optimize product rotation for freshness
- **Order Fulfillment**: Process online orders, coordinate picking and packing, manage delivery routes, and track customer satisfaction
