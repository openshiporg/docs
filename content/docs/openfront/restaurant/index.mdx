## Key Flows

### Customer Experience
- **Online Ordering**: Browse menu, customize orders, schedule pickup/delivery, and track order status with real-time updates
- **Table Reservations**: Check availability, book tables, manage party size, and receive confirmation with special requests handling
- **Loyalty & Reviews**: Earn points, redeem rewards, leave feedback, and access exclusive member promotions

### Restaurant Operations
- **Kitchen Management**: Process orders efficiently, coordinate with front-of-house, manage inventory levels, and track preparation times
- **Staff Coordination**: Schedule shifts, assign tables, coordinate delivery drivers, and manage peak hour operations
