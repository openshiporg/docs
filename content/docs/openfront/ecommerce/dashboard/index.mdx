The dashboard lets you manage your entire e-commerce platform. It's built with Next.js 15 and KeystoneJS 6, giving you a modern, responsive interface for business operations.

## Dashboard Navigation

The dashboard is organized into focused sections for efficient management:

### Core Operations
- **Orders** - Complete order management, returns, claims, and fulfillment tracking
- **Analytics** - Sales reports, customer insights, and business performance metrics
- **Markets** - Multi-regional settings including regions, currencies, countries, and shipping

### Products & Catalog
- **Products** - Product catalog with variants, inventory, and pricing management
- **Categories** - Product category hierarchy and organization
- **Collections** - Marketing-focused product groupings and themed collections

### Customers & Users
- **Users** - Customer accounts, administrative users, and permission management

### Marketing & Growth
- **Discounts** - Promotional codes, discount rules, and campaign management
- **Price Lists** - Customer-specific pricing and bulk pricing strategies
- **Gift Cards** - Gift card creation, redemption tracking, and balance management
- **Shipping Options** - Customer-facing shipping methods and pricing

### Integrations
- **Apps** - Third-party applications and integrations
- **Shipping Providers** - Configure Shippo and other shipping integrations
- **Payment Providers** - Set up Stripe, PayPal, and other payment gateways

## Key Features

### Modern Interface
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Intuitive Navigation**: Organized sidebar with collapsible sections for efficient workflow
- **Advanced Filtering**: Search and filter capabilities across all data management screens
- **Bulk Operations**: Mass actions for efficient data management

### Business Management
- **Real-time Analytics**: Live sales data, revenue tracking, and performance insights
- **Order Processing**: Complete order lifecycle from creation to fulfillment
- **Inventory Management**: Stock tracking with multi-location support
- **Customer Relations**: User accounts, permissions, and customer data management

### System Administration  
- **Multi-Regional Support**: Manage currencies, countries, and regional settings
- **Provider Integration**: Configure payment and shipping providers with webhook support
- **Security & Permissions**: Role-based access control with audit trails
- **API Management**: Generate API keys and manage external integrations

### Advanced Models
For developers and advanced users, the dashboard provides direct access to all underlying data models through the Models section, including detailed management of orders, products, customers, payments, shipping, and system configurations.

## Getting Started

1. **Access your dashboard** at `/dashboard` after installation
2. **Configure basic settings** including store information and regional preferences  
3. **Set up integrations** for payments and shipping providers
4. **Add your product catalog** with categories and collections
5. **Start processing orders** and managing your business operations
