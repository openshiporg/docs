## Openfront Dealership

Openfront Dealership empowers automotive retailers with complete control over their vehicle sales and service operations without surrendering control to expensive automotive platform subscriptions.

## Key Flows

### Customer Experience
- **Vehicle Shopping**: Browse inventory, compare models, view detailed specifications, and schedule test drives with preferred sales representatives
- **Financing & Purchase**: Apply for financing, compare loan options, complete paperwork digitally, and track purchase progress with transparent pricing
- **Service & Maintenance**: Schedule service appointments, track vehicle history, receive maintenance reminders, and access warranty information

### Dealership Operations
- **Inventory Management**: Track vehicle stock, manage trade-ins, coordinate with manufacturers, and optimize lot layout for maximum sales efficiency
- **Sales Pipeline**: Manage leads, track customer interactions, coordinate test drives, and monitor sales performance with detailed analytics
