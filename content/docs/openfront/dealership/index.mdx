## Openfront Dealership

Openfront Dealership empowers automotive retailers with complete control over their vehicle sales and service operations without surrendering control to expensive automotive platform subscriptions.

<Steps>
<Step>
### Storefront
Customer-facing vehicle showroom with listings, specifications, and test drive booking.
</Step>

<Step>
### Dashboard
Dealership operations system for inventory management, sales pipeline, and service scheduling.
</Step>

<Step>
### Marketplace
Connect with other dealerships to create inventory networks and vehicle trade programs.
</Step>
</Steps>
