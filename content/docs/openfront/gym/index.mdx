Openfront Gym puts fitness businesses back in control with comprehensive membership and class management tools designed specifically for gyms, studios, and wellness centers.

## Key Flows

### Member Experience
- **Sign Up & Onboarding**: Members can easily register, select membership plans, and complete profile setup with fitness goals and preferences
- **Class Booking**: Browse available classes, check instructor profiles, book spots, and receive reminders with automatic waitlist management
- **Membership Management**: Track usage, upgrade/downgrade plans, manage payment methods, and access exclusive member benefits

### Gym Operations
- **Staff Coordination**: Schedule trainers, manage class capacity, coordinate equipment maintenance, and track member check-ins
- **Business Analytics**: Monitor membership growth, class popularity, revenue tracking, and member retention metrics
